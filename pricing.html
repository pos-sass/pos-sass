Our Philosophy & Your Questions

Transparency is key. Here's what to expect and answers to common questions.

Future Pricing Philosophy

Our goal is to create the best POS solution for retail businesses. While POS Pro is free to use during the Early Access Program, we plan to introduce transparent and fair pricing tiers in the future to support ongoing development, server costs, and new advanced features.

Rest assured, we will provide ample notice (at least 30-60 days) before any pricing changes. Early adopters like you will always be valued, and we often provide special considerations or discounts for those who help us grow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is POS Pro really free right now?

Yes! During our Early Access Program, POS Pro is completely free to use with access to all currently available features. We want your feedback to make it perfect.

What is expected of me as an Early Access user?

We encourage you to use POS Pro for your daily retail operations. Your primary role is to provide feedback on usability, report any bugs you encounter, and suggest improvements. Your active usage is invaluable.

Will I have to pay when new features like Bulk Upload or AI scanning are added?

During the Early Access Program, you'll likely get to test many new features for free as they are introduced for testing and refinement. We will be transparent about any future pricing models for advanced features well in advance.

What happens if POS Pro introduces pricing I don't agree with or if the service is decommissioned?

We are committed to transparency and fairness. As outlined in our Terms & Conditions, if we transition to a paid model you don't wish to adopt, or in the unlikely event we decide to decommission the service, we will provide a mechanism for you to download your core business data (like product lists, sales history, inventory data) in a common format.
