Explore POS Pro Features

Discover the comprehensive suite of tools POS Pro offers to revolutionize your retail management, from seamless sales to powerful analytics.

Sales & Checkout

Effortless Sales & Operations

Speed through transactions and manage daily tasks with unparalleled ease.

Intuitive POS Interface

Fast, easy-to-use sales screen with product grid (category-tabbed), quick product search (name, SKU, barcode), and touch-friendly design for rapid checkouts.

Flexible Order Management

Handle multiple concurrent orders efficiently with "Hold" and "Resume" functionality, ensuring no sale is missed during peak hours.

Professional Invoicing

Generate clear, 80mm thermal printer-formatted bills with store address and GSTIN, enhancing your brand's professionalism.

Store Management

Comprehensive Back-Office

Control every aspect of your store from a centralized and powerful dashboard.

User Roles & Permissions

Secure access with distinct roles (Admin, Manager, Cashier) and granular permissions for data security and operational control.

Product Cataloguing

Detailed product management including name, description, price, SKU, barcode, images, and active status for a rich catalog.

Category Management

Organize products into hierarchical categories specific to each store, simplifying navigation and reporting.

Stock Management

Advanced Inventory Control

Maintain perfect stock levels and minimize losses with our robust inventory tools.

Real-time Stock Tracking

Monitor quantity on hand in real-time across all your products and locations to prevent stockouts or overstocking.

AI Invoice Scanning

Analytics

Powerful Insights & Reporting

Make informed decisions with comprehensive, real-time data and customizable reports.

Role-Based Dashboards

Admin, Manager, and Cashier views for tailored insights into system-wide, store-specific, or individual performance.

Detailed Reports

Access sales summaries, user performance, store comparisons, best-selling products, and category-wise sales reports.
