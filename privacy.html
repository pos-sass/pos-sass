Welcome to POS Pro ("us", "we", or "our") available at pos-sass.com. We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason. We will alert you about any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect via the Service includes:

Personal Data

Personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and business name, that you voluntarily give to us when you register for the Service, request a demo, or contact us.

Business Data

Information related to your retail business that you input into POS Pro, such as product details (name, description, price, SKU, barcode, images), sales transactions, inventory data (quantity on hand, stock adjustments), category information, customer information (if you use the optional customer management feature, e.g., name, mobile), user/employee data for access control, and store configuration details. This data is owned by you.

Derivative Data

Information our servers automatically collect when you access the Service, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you have viewed directly before and after accessing the Service. If you are using our application, this information may also include device information and usage statistics for the purpose of improving the Service and ensuring stability.

Having accurate information permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Service to:

  • Create and manage your account and provide the POS Pro services.
  • Process your transactions and send you related information, including confirmations and invoices (if applicable).
  • Enable you to manage your business operations within POS Pro (e.g., sales, inventory, reporting).
  • Communicate with you, including responding to your inquiries, providing support, and sending administrative messages.
  • Improve the efficiency, operation, and stability of the Service.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with the Service.
  • Notify you of updates to the Service.
  • Offer new products, services, and/or recommendations to you (with your consent where required).
  • Prevent fraudulent transactions, monitor against theft, and protect against criminal activity.
  • Resolve disputes and troubleshoot problems.
